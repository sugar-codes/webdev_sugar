Bread Omlette

Hello guys! In this page, we'll be learning how to make a delicious bread omlette for your healthy breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • A packet of fresh bread

    • Instructions

    1. In a bowl, pour the contents of the egg.
    2. Add chilli powder and salt to it.
    3. Add the chopped onion to it.
    4. Mix all the ingredients well, and a batter will be ready.
    5. Pour this batter on a boiling pan and let it fry.
    6. Your omlette is ready. Put the omlette between the bread slices and make a sandwich.
    7. Your bread omlette is ready!
